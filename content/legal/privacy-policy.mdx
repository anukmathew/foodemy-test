Welcome to **Foodemy**. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit [https://foodemy.in](https://foodemy.in) (the "Site").

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our Site:

### a. Personal Information

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Payment details (collected securely through third-party payment processors)

### b. Non-Personal Information

- Browser type
- Device information
- IP address
- Pages visited and time spent on the Site

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

- Provide and improve our services and content.
- Process course enrollments and payments.
- Communicate important updates, offers, and promotional content (only with your consent).
- Respond to your inquiries and provide support.

## 3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do **NOT** sell or share your personal data with third parties, except in the following cases:

- With trusted third-party service providers (such as payment gateways and analytics providers) who help us operate the Site and process payments.
- To comply with legal obligations (e.g., government requests).

## 4. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on the Site. Cookies help us:

- Keep you logged in during your visit.
- Analyze Site traffic and performance.
- Personalize content based on your preferences.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the Site may not work properly.

## 5. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the personal information we hold about you.
- Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
- Withdraw consent for direct marketing.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at **support@foodemy.in**.

## 7. Children’s Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on the Site. Your continued use of the Site indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

## 9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

**Email**: support@foodemy.in  
**Website**: [https://foodemy.in](https://foodemy.in)
