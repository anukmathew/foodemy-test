## No Refund Policy

All payments made for courses and services on [https://foodemy.in](https://foodemy.in) are **strictly non-refundable**.

When you make a payment, you acknowledge and agree that:

- You have read the course description carefully.
- You understand the course content and structure before enrolling.

## Exceptional Cases

In very rare cases where exceptional circumstances arise, you may contact us to request a refund. Please note:

- Any refund requests will be reviewed at our sole discretion.
- Approval of refunds is not guaranteed and is considered only in exceptional situations.
- We may request supporting evidence for your refund request.

## How to Contact Us

If you believe you qualify for a refund under exceptional circumstances, please reach out to us with full details at:

**Email**: [support@foodemy.in](mailto:support@foodemy.in)  
**Website**: [https://foodemy.in](https://foodemy.in)  
**Mobile Number**: [+918076828540](tel:8076828540)

## Final Decision

The decision to approve or deny a refund request lies entirely at the discretion of **Foodemy** management.

**_Our decision will be final and binding._**
